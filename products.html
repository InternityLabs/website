Help Frequently Asked Questions

What is Win-X Launcher?

Win-X Launcher is a Windows-style Android launcher for phones and tablets, focused on a familiar desktop-like experience with customization options such as widgets, shortcuts, icon packs, and multiple desktops.

What’s the difference between Win-X Launcher, Foldables, and Ultimate?

Win-X Launcher is the standard edition. Win-X Launcher for Foldables is tuned for foldable devices with independent configuration for the cover and main screens. Win-X Launcher Ultimate is an upfront purchase edition for users who prefer paying once rather than using in-app purchase options.

Does Win-X Launcher collect personal data?

Win-X Launcher does not collect personal data. If the app encounters a crash or serious error, it may generate a limited crash report to help diagnose and fix the problem. See our Privacy Policy for details.
