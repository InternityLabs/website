Types of Data collected

If case of certain types of error during the operation of the app, the following data is collected by the app and sent over to Internity Labs.

* Version of Android on your device.

* Device Language

* Version of the Win-X Launcher app on your device

* exception trace of the error from our code running on your device.

Method of log data collection

The app programmatically sends an email to a gmail account associated with the InternityLabs.

Mode and place of processing the log Data

Methods of processing

The Owner takes appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or unauthorized destruction of the log Data.

The log data is regularly screened by persons in charge, involved with the operation of this Application (System administration) for any issues which can be fixed or improved in the future releases of the app. Data is also be screened when some user reaches out to the Internity Labs for any problem based by their app installation.

Place

The log data is processed at the Owner's operating office. Depending on the User's location, data transfers may involve transferring the User's Data to a country other than their own.

Retention time

The log Data shall be processed and stored for as long as required by the purpose they have been collected for.

Data which is not collected

App requests permission to certain features from your mobile device, including your mobile device's bluetooth, microphone, storage, notifications, location, and other features, but we don't collect and receive this information. It is only used by the app on your device, and it never leaves the boundary of your device.

Changes to this privacy policy

Since we don't collect any personal information about the app's users we will not be able to inform the users about the privacy policy changes.
